<idea>
  **Design Movement**: "Neo-Brutalist Utility"
  **Core Principles**:
  1. **Raw Functionality**: Exposed grids, visible structural lines, and high-contrast borders.
  2. **Bold Typography**: Large, impactful headings using a monospaced or heavy sans-serif font.
  3. **High Contrast**: Stark black and white base with vibrant, almost neon accent colors (electric blue, safety yellow).
  4. **Information Density**: Layouts designed to show maximum data without clutter, using clear separators.

  **Color Philosophy**:
  - **Base**: #000000 (Black), #FFFFFF (White)
  - **Accents**: #3B82F6 (Bright Blue), #FCD34D (Safety Yellow)
  - **Intent**: Reflects the "operating system" metaphor—raw, powerful, and tool-like. It feels like a command center for serious work.

  **Layout Paradigm**:
  - **Modular Grid**: Strict, visible grid lines separating content blocks.
  - **Sidebar Navigation**: Fixed, high-contrast sidebar with bold icons.
  - **Card-Based Content**: Content lives in clearly defined cards with heavy borders and drop shadows (no blur, hard edges).

  **Signature Elements**:
  - **Hard Shadows**: Buttons and cards have solid, offset shadows (e.g., `box-shadow: 4px 4px 0px #000`).
  - **Marquee Text**: Scrolling text for announcements or status updates.
  - **Monospace Data**: Dates, tags, and metrics displayed in a coding font.

  **Interaction Philosophy**:
  - **Tactile Feedback**: Buttons depress visually on click (transform: translate).
  - **Hover States**: Elements invert colors or shift position sharply on hover.

  **Animation**:
  - **Snappy Transitions**: Instant or very fast ease-out transitions (no soft fades).
  - **Glitch Effects**: Subtle glitch animations on hover or load for a tech-forward feel.

  **Typography System**:
  - **Headings**: 'JetBrains Mono' or 'Space Mono' (Bold, Uppercase).
  - **Body**: 'Inter' or 'Public Sans' for readability.
</idea>

  **Core Principles**:
  1. **Translucency**: Frosted glass effects (backdrop-filter: blur) for panels and overlays.

  - **Base**: #F0F9FF (Alice Blue), #FFFFFF (White)
  - **Accents**: #60A5FA (Light Blue), #818CF8 (Indigo), #34D399 (Emerald)
  - **Intent**: Evokes the "future" and "innovation" aspects of the startup ecosystem. It feels modern, fluid, and inspiring.

  **Layout Paradigm**:
  - **Fluid Containers**: Rounded corners (large radii) and soft boundaries.
  - **Centralized Focus**: Key content takes center stage with peripheral elements fading back.
  - **Overlay Navigation**: Floating navigation bar or sidebar with glass effect.

  **Signature Elements**:
  - **Glass Cards**: White backgrounds with low opacity and blur.
  - **Mesh Gradients**: Subtle, colorful gradients used as backgrounds or within text.
  - **Glow Effects**: Soft outer glows on active elements.

  **Interaction Philosophy**:
  - **Fluidity**: Smooth, spring-based animations for all interactions.
  - **Depth**: Hover states lift elements closer to the user (scale + shadow).

  **Animation**:
  - **Parallax**: Subtle movement of background elements relative to foreground.
  - **Fade & Slide**: Soft entrances for content.

  **Typography System**:
  - **Headings**: 'Plus Jakarta Sans' (Geometric, Friendly).
  - **Body**: 'DM Sans' or 'Outfit'.

<idea>
  **Design Movement**: "Swiss International Style (Digital)"
  **Core Principles**:
  1. **Objective Clarity**: Content is king; design serves to organize and present data without decoration.
  2. **Asymmetric Balance**: Dynamic layouts that use whitespace to balance heavy elements.
  3. **Grid Systems**: Mathematical precision in alignment and spacing.
  4. **Typography as Image**: Large, bold typography used as a primary visual element.

  **Color Philosophy**:
  - **Base**: #FFFFFF (White), #F3F4F6 (Light Gray)
  - **Accents**: #1E3A8A (Navy Blue), #EF4444 (Red - for alerts/actions)
  - **Intent**: Professional, trustworthy, and efficient. Aligns with the "hub" concept—organized and reliable.

  **Layout Paradigm**:
  - **Asymmetric Grid**: Content spans multiple columns; whitespace is used actively.
  - **Horizontal Rhythm**: Strong horizontal lines and alignment.
  - **Typographic Hierarchy**: Size and weight define importance, not color or decoration.

  **Signature Elements**:
  - **Heavy Rules**: Thick horizontal lines separating sections.
  - **Oversized Numbers**: Metrics displayed in massive font sizes.
  - **Minimal Icons**: Simple, geometric icons (or none at all).

  **Interaction Philosophy**:
  - **Precision**: Instant, clear feedback. No unnecessary motion.
  - **Focus**: Interactions highlight the active data point or tool.

  **Animation**:
  - **Minimal**: Only functional transitions (e.g., accordion expand/collapse).
  - **Linear**: Simple, linear movements.

  **Typography System**:
  - **Headings**: 'Helvetica Now' or 'Inter' (Tight tracking, Heavy weights).
  - **Body**: 'Inter' or 'Roboto' (Clean, legible).
</idea>
</text>
